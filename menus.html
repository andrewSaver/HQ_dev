THE HINDQUARTER

Sample Menus

At The Hindquarter we cook all our food over fire fueled by hardwoods from our land in Huntington, VT. We have custom Argentinian style grills crafted by local welder Mark Shattuck .

When planning an event, we will advise our clients to choose food that utilizes all of our grills. We sear on our chapas, we bake in the infiernillo, we grill over low coals on the ring of fire, we roast on our skewers and swords.

Please take a look at our sample menus to give you a sense of the possible options for the food at your event. Every event has its own customized menu and we're very open to modifications and menu item feedback.

We also pull our smoker to our barbeque events.
