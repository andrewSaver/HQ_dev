Wedding and Event Planning

Many clients wonder what the role of a catering company is when planning an event. The Hindquarter will help walk you through menu design, rental orders, vendor organization, and execution of your event. We offer simple and unique meals for weddings, company events, small dinner parties, or any other event at which a prepared meal is desired.

The first step is emailing us through our contact page . We will respond with a full list of menus for you to look over. This will be followed up with a phone call to discuss other details that may affect your menu and quote. We'll offer a proposal and then adjust to make sure our clients are comfortable with our services and costs. The date is secured and we start the process of planning your event in detail.

We have Event Coordinators who meet with the client to discuss all details prior to the event. Needs vary greatly from location to location and many venues require rentals. Our coordinators manage the rental list, all details and the time-frame specific to the event. Most of the time, venues need to be ‘assembled’. Whether it be a tented wedding at Huntington River Vineyard or a private dinner party, tables, chairs and tabletop decor need to be managed.

Occasionally, clients want to tend to some of these details themselves, which is always fine. From the custom designed menu to the set-up and break-down of the event, every detail will be taken care of.

We also pull our smoker trailer to barbeque events for more casual North Carolina style slow cooked brisket, pulled pork, ribs, chicken, and side dishes.
