The Hindquarter is equipped with a large, tow-behind smoker that can accommodate parties of up to 300 guests. We offer a “Vermont spin" on southern barbeque, using local meats and vegetables. The Hindquarter can simply drop off a BBQ meal ready to eat or handle the set up, service, and clean up at your event. We look forward to working with you!

The Feast

All catered meals come with house made parker house rolls, pickles and extra BBQ sauces. Drop off orders are brought in aluminum trays, catered meals are served on our own platters

Snacks

If you want something for your guests to snack on during a cocktail hour, find it here. For drop-off events, understand that the food will be arriving hot, so you should be ready to eat when we arrive. If you want to enjoy “snacks” before eating the main meal at these events, your food will be cooling down! Depending on choices you can expect snacks to be $3-9/person
