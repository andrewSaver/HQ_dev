The Smoker

In addition to wedding and event catering using our Argentinian grills, we also offer our clients traditional North Carolina style barbeque items that are slow cooked in our wood smoker trailer. We begin the process the day before your event, and time the cooking so that the meat is just finishing as we tow the trailer to the event site. As with all of our events, we use ingredients from local farms and make everything from scratch. Whether your event is a family barbeque or a larger gathering, we can provide an authentic and memorable barbeque experience.
