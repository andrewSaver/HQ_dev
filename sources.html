Source and Venue Partners

Photo credit: Wilde Scout Photo Co.

At The Hindquarter we work with several local partners who provide our ingredients and host our client's events. From the farmers who provide our animals and vegetables to the property owners who provide wedding and event venues, we feel fortunate to have the relationships that allow us to cook locally sourced food and to present it in a variety of beautiful locations. Here's a list of some of our Vermont connections with whom we work year round.

If you haven't already committed to a venue for your wedding or event, we encourage you to get in touch with any of the below venue locations to see what dates they may have open. They are folks with whom we've worked in the past and we know that they're able to accommodate our space requirements for our grills and equipment.

Farms

Half Pint Farm

We proudly buy a wide variety of organic seasonal produce from Half Pint Farm in Burlington's intervale.

Visit Half Pint Farm
Jericho Settler's Farm

We purchase organic produce all season from our partners at Jericho Settler's Farm in Jericho, Vermont.

Visit Jericho Settlers Farm
Vermont Heritage Grazers

We purchase whole, GMO-free Berkshire pigs from Vermont Heritage Grazers in Bridport, Vermont. We use them for our pig roasts, sausages, hams, and charcuterie.

Vermont Heritage Grazers
Philo Ridge Farm

We purchase organic produce, chicken, and grass fed beef from Philo Ridge Farm in Charlotte, Vermont.

Visit Philo Ridge
Boyden Farm Beef

We buy grass fed beef from Boyden Farm in Cambridge, Vermont for our weddings and barbeques.

Visit Boyden Farm
Pete's Greens

Pete's Greens in Craftsbury, Vermont supplies us with organic greens and produce all year long from their fields and greenhouses.

Visit Pete's Greens

Venues

Bliss Ridge Farm
Moretown, VT

Bliss Ridge Farm in Moretown, Vermont has hosted many of our client's weddings on their beautiful working farm and in their historic barn.

Visit Bliss Ridge
The Barns at Lang Farm
Essex Junction, VT

The Barns at Lang Farm in Essex Junction, Vermont are a pair of separate venues located on an 18 hole golf course and set among perennial gardens. The Garden Barn and Main Barn are both great locations for a wedding that are relatively close to Burlington and the airport.

Visit Lang Farm
The Alerin Barn
St. Johnsbury, VT

The Alerin Barn in St. Johnsbury, Vermont is a pastoral estate in Vermont's Northeast Kingdom. It has a well-appointed manor house for wedding party guests and a timber frame barn for wedding receptions.

Visit Alerin Barn
All Souls Interfaith Gathering
Shelburne, VT

All Souls Interfaith Gathering is a gorgeous site located on an elevated ledge overlooking Lake Champlain and the Adirondacks. They provide a modern facility for amenities while guests enjoy the beauty of the property outdoors.

Visit All Souls
Sleepy Hollow Inn
Huntington, VT

Sleepy Hollow Inn in Huntington, Vermont is an inn with a round barn and pavilion with mountain views set on a network of wooded trails.

Visit Half Pint
Windekind Farm
Huntington, VT

Windekind Farm in Huntington, Vermont is a site that offers three finished cottages, farm buildings, meadows, ponds, and a 1/5 size garden railroad. It provides a beautiful setting and views.

Visit Windekind Farm
The Barn at Boyden Farm
Cambridge, VT

The farm and vineyard at The Barn at Boyden Farm is a great setting for a wedding in Cambridge, Vermont. We also buy their grass fed beef for many of our weddings and events.

Visit Half Pint
Isham Family Farm
Williston, VT

Isham Family Farm in Williston, Vermont provides a meticulously restored former dairy barn for wedding receptions, as well as a stone patio, farm pond, and mountain views.

Visit Isham Family Farm
The West Monitor Barn
Richmond, VT

The West Monitor Barn in Richmond, Vermont is a beautifully restored landmark barn with spectacular views of forests, woodlands, and conserved fields in the foothills of Camel's Hump mountain.

Visit West Monitor Barn
Huntington River Vineyard
Huntington, VT

Huntington River Vineyard in Huntington, Vermont is set in an idyllic valley vineyard in the foothills of Camel's Hump mountain. A rustic barn, open fields, and a working vineyard provide the scenery. It's a perfect spot for The Hindquarter to cater your event.

Visit Huntington River Vineyard
